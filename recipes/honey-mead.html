Homemade Honey Mead (Non-Alcoholic)

A sweet and refreshing drink inspired by Viking feasts, made with honey and spices — no fermentation required.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter filtered water
  • 3–4 tablespoons raw honey (to taste)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 small slice of fresh ginger
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced
  • Optional: fresh mint or rosemary for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat the water gently (do not boil).
  2. Add the cinnamon, cloves, ginger, and lemon slices.
  3. Simmer for about 10 minutes to infuse the flavors.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the honey until dissolved.
  5. Let it cool to room temperature, then strain.
  6. Serve chilled or over ice. Garnish with mint or rosemary if desired.
