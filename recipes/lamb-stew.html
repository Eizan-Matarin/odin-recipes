Hearty Viking Lamb Stew

A rustic stew inspired by traditional Norse ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 parsnips, chopped
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 100g barley
  • 1 liter vegetable or bone broth
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp animal fat or butter

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the fat over medium heat. Brown the lamb on all sides.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the carrots, parsnips, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the barley and broth, bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, until the lamb is tender and the stew is thickened.
  6. Adjust seasoning and serve hot, preferably with rustic bread.
