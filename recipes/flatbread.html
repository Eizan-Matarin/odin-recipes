Scandinavian Flatbread

A thin, crispy bread enjoyed across the Nordic lands — perfect with stew, cheese, or fish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (or barley/rye mix for authenticity)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter or lard (optional)
  • 2/3 cup water (adjust as needed)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the butter if using and mix well.
  2. Gradually stir in the water until a smooth dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough briefly, then divide into 6–8 equal balls.
  4. Roll each ball into a very thin circle (the thinner, the crispier).
  5. Cook on a dry skillet or griddle over medium heat for 1–2 minutes on each side, until lightly browned and crisp.
  6. Cool completely before storing. Serve plain or with toppings like smoked fish, butter, or cheese.
