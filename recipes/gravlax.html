Nordic Gravlax

A traditional Scandinavian delicacy of cured salmon with dill, perfect for feasts or light meals.

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh salmon fillet, skin on
  • 2 tablespoons coarse sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon crushed black pepper
  • 1 bunch fresh dill, roughly chopped
  • Optional: a splash of vodka or aquavit (for authentic flavor)

Instructions

  1. Mix the salt, sugar, and black pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Rub the mixture all over the salmon, both sides.
  3. Place half the chopped dill in a dish, lay the salmon skin-side down, and top with the rest of the dill.
  4. Optionally sprinkle a small splash of vodka or aquavit over the top.
  5. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap and place a weight on top (like a heavy plate).
  6. Refrigerate for 24–48 hours, turning the fish every 12 hours.
  7. When ready, remove the dill, slice the salmon thinly, and serve cold with rye bread or mustard sauce.
